Welcome to the BBD Minecraft Server Registration

In order to access the BBD Minecraft Server, you need to verify that you are an employee of BBD.

The easiest way to do this is to check that you are part of the BBD Gaming Discord server.

Please ensure that your minecraft username is correct and click the button to verify with Discord.

This verification process uses Discord's OAuth2 flow. You will sign in with your discord account so That we can verify that you are part of the discord server. the scope used for this sign in is only identify and guilds, we will not access or store any other information.
