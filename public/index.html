Welcome to the BBD Minecraft Server Registration

In order to access the BBD Minecraft Server, you need to verify that you are an employee of BBD.

The easiest way to do this is to send a verification code to your BBD email address.

Please ensure that your minecraft username is correct and enter your BBD email address. We will send you a verification code that you can then enter to whitelist your Minecraft account on the server.

Note: We will never use you email address for anything other than sending the verification code.
